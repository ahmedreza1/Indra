Batteries

We, at Indra Battery, are specializing in various products related to battery. We take extra care in providing quality and efficiency in our products. We maintain that our batteries are durable, cost-effective and requires low maintenance.

P. P. Automotive Batteries

We design and manufacture a wide range of P.P. automotive batteries that come with well-designed 'positive plates' which are capable of producing high power and voltage. These batteries have especially designed negative plates for providing a perfect balance along with positive. This helps in increasing the performance of the batteries.

Our batteries are offered with glass mat separator that protects against active material shedding at the time of vibration which assures longer service life. The fine pores of poly vinyl chlorine separators are helpful in the free movement of electrolyte between the two plates that are positive and negative. This also helps in eliminating the internal short circuits that takes place within the cell. The plate density helps in providing increased plate strength. Our range of P.P. Automotive batteries are used in the following applications:

  • Car & Truck
  • Telecommunications & Telephone exchanges
  • Emergency lighting
  • Genset Starting Operations
  • EPABX
  • Alarm Systems

Inverter/UPS batteries

Our inverters/UPS batteries are available with well designed 'positive plates' that produce high power and voltage. These batteries are especially designed negative plates that provide a perfect balance along with positive plates. Our batteries are offered with PG separator that protects against shedding of active material during vibration that assures high performance during service life. The plate density provides increased plate strength. Our range of batteries are used in inverters, UPS & solar system are offered in three different types that include:

  • INVASTAR Inverter/Stand By Application Series (Tubular Plate)
  • INVASTAR Inverter batteries - Tubular Plate

Industrial Batteries

We design and manufacture a wide range of industrial batteries that is tubular positive plate and negative plates. The positive plates come with grids that are cast from special alloy that give high mechanical strength. We use gauntlet tubes that are of high bursting strength as oxide holders. The balanced ratio of grid to positive active material helps in yielding better performance and leads to longer service life. Each plate undergoes strict quality surveillance to ensure consistent and superior performance.

The design construction of negative plate is balanced with equivalent positive plates that helps in increasing performance. Special alloy and paste formation helps in enhancing the characteristics. The perfect curing method and process ensures proper binding of active material to grid thus totally eliminating shedding phenomena.

Our range of industrial batteries are use in UPS & inverter system, telecommunications and telephone, exchanges, railway applications, emergency lighting, genset starting operations, EPABX, alarm system, power transmission.

Lead Alloy

We offer lead alloys in two varieties, namely:

ANTIMONIAL LEAD Our range of lead antimony alloy is mainly used for manufacturing grids, straps and terminals of lead acid batteries. We specialize in producing lead alloys containing 2.5% to 3.5% antimony and 3.0% to 3.5% of normal gravity.

SELENIUM ALLOY We produce lead-selenium alloy for low maintenance batteries as well as other type of batteries. It has the unique feature of reducing the consumption of water, making batteries more easy to use, with minimum maintenance. This is making selenium alloy very popular among the clients along with constant increase in demand for them.
